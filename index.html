ABOUT ME


I am a computational engineer student at the Federal University of Paraiba (UFPB). My studies and works are focused on the internet of things (IoT) and development, I love entrepreneurship and innovation.
Had my first contact with programming when I studied at a technical school (Federal Institute of Alagoas), where I quickly fell in love with the field and jumped headfirst into the technology world.
There I had the opportunity to deepen my knowledge of programming and robotics. I had before various experiences of research and labor market, in highlight the robot Toddy (affectionate nickname given to the robot that belongs to the programming learning computational tool), which gave me a lot of experience and knowledge on mobile programming and robotics.

Toddy Teddy Jhonny

The name was given to the robot built by me and a few colleagues, the result of a research project. The robot is capable of following instructions sent by the app, also developed by our team. Gymbro is an application for bodybuilders. It was developed with Flutter and with this is possible to have new partnes to workout in gym, share experiences and knowledge.
